Design Elements

Shapes should maintain a strong, geometric form while also honoring the soft and friendly personality of the site. The majority of the designs are based on round shapes such as circles, cylinders, and ovals. These are not suggestions and are crucial in order to maintain the mood and personality of the site’s styling.
